## Agentic Engineering

Full apps built end-to-end with AI agents.

- 🔁 **[stuck-problems-agent](https://github.com/Monte9/stuck-problems-agent)** - Autonomous research loop for stuck problems, run on a schedule by Claude Code.
- 🏛️ **[history-stories](https://github.com/Monte9/history-stories)** - A 3D museum you walk through in the browser, every painting a short story from history. ([live](https://historystories.vercel.app))
- ⚔️ **[claw-debates](https://github.com/Monte9/claw-debates)** - AI vs AI debate arena: two LLMs argue any topic, with an automated judge and audience voting. ([live](https://clawdebates.vercel.app))
- 🧠 **[quiz-me](https://github.com/Monte9/quiz-me)** - Multi-user trivia where Claude quizzes you on topics you pick and grades honestly. ([live](https://quizmenexus.vercel.app))
- 📚 **[deep-research-agent](https://github.com/Monte9/deep-research-agent)** - Long-form research essays co-written with an agent: brainstorm → research → draft → publish.
- 📊 **[cursor-stats](https://github.com/Monte9/cursor-stats)** - Upload your Cursor usage CSV, ask questions in plain English, get charts — data stays in your browser. ([live](https://cursorstats.vercel.app))
- ⛳ **[math-golf-game](https://github.com/Monte9/math-golf-game)** - Puzzle game where math operations are golf clubs: hit the target in as few strokes as possible. ([live](https://mathgolfgame.vercel.app))

_More on the way as I open-source them._
