## Soft notes

- Design is minimal and consistent: system fonts, generous whitespace, semantic-token chrome; the Sunset theme
  reads as an intentional warm palette (cream bg, terracotta accent), not garish. Good.
- Settings layout is clean and the active-theme treatment (accent ring + accent label) is unambiguous.
- Pin targets remain small (radius 0.022); a casual user may need a couple of hover attempts — same minor friction
  noted in the prior globe sprint. Not a fail (drag + dense hover both reveal pins reliably).
- Switch knob is hardcoded white (`bg-white`); fine on the accent "on" state and on the surface-2 "off" state in
  all themes (visible in screenshots), but it is the one nav/control color not derived from a token. Cosmetic only.
- Screenshot filenames `travel-motionON-*` / `travel-motionOFF-*` are labeled by the test's intent and are inverted
  relative to the reduce-motion state (motionOFF files = reduce-motion ON / static). Conclusions in S2-AC3 use the data.
