## Soft notes (non-failing)

- Visited countries have empty blurbs by data design (`blurb: ""`); the surface
  conditionally omits the blurb line, so no empty paragraph renders — correct.
- Surface opens on click/tap only; hover emphasizes the country but does not open the
  surface. AC1 says "Clicking/tapping… opens", so this is compliant. (GOAL mentions
  "hovering or tapping reveals rich info" as an aspiration; hover-to-open could be a
  nice future enhancement on desktop but is not required by the agreed AC.)
- Hover/focus/active states present: close button has hover styles; cursor switches to
  pointer over fills; dialog receives focus on open.
