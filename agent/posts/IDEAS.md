## Domain space (pick + dedup; scout adjacent stalled problems)

regulatory move go" angle: public health & healthcare delivery, climate &
environment, energy & the grid, oceans & fisheries/IUU, space debris, child
safety, evidence integrity / clinical trials, immigration & labor, housing,
education access. Prefer a sharp, decision-oriented question over a survey.
